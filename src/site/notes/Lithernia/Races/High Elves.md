# High Elves

High Elves,  the most noble and powerful of the elven races,  are known for their mastery of magic,  their wisdom,  and their elegant cities.  They are a proud and ancient people,  with a deep connection to the history and traditions of Eldrador.  High Elves are skilled in the arts of diplomacy,  warfare,  and magic,  and they are often seen as leaders and guardians of the elven people.  They value beauty,  knowledge,  and order,  and they strive to maintain balance and harmony in the world.
